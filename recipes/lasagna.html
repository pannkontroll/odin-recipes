Home-made Lasagna, from kitchen to plate:

To make the classic Lasagna recipes that humans have enjoyed for hundreds of years, you won't need much.

Pasta plates, ground beef, and some bechámel sauce are the key players in this fantastic recipe.

Ingredients Required:

  • Ground beef
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Bechámel sauce
  • Pasta plates/pasta noodles
  • Parmesan cheese
  • White sugar
  • Herbs (Parsley, basil, black pepper)

How To Make Lasagna:

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Cook the plates
  3. Layer the lasagna
  4. Put the stuff on the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
  6. Eat the lasagna
